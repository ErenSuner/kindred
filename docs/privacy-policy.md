# Kindred — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 21 July 2026_

Kindred ("the app") helps you remember the people you care about — their
birthdays, special days, and your notes and photos about them. This policy
explains what the app collects, why, and the choices you have. Kindred is a
personal app for a single account; there is no sharing, no social layer, and no
selling of data.

## Who is responsible

Kindred is operated by Eren Suner. For any privacy question or request, contact
**erenn.suner@gmail.com**.

## What we collect

We only collect what you enter or choose to import:

- **Account data** — your email address and, optionally, your name and a profile
  photo. Used to sign you in and label your account.
- **People and occasions** — the names, relationships, birthdays, special days,
  events, and reminders you add.
- **Notes and photos** — gift ideas, memories, notebook entries, and any images
  you attach.
- **Contacts you choose to import** — if you use "Import from contacts", only the
  contacts you select are read (name and, where present, birthday). We do not
  upload your whole address book, and importing is always your explicit action.
- **Photos you choose to add** — pictures you pick from your device's photo
  library to attach to a person or a memory.

We do **not** collect location, advertising identifiers, browsing history, or
analytics about how you tap around the app.

## Device permissions

- **Contacts** — only to let you import people you select. Optional.
- **Photos / media library** — only to let you pick an image for a person or a
  memory. Optional.
- **Notifications** — to deliver the birthday and event reminders you set up.
  Reminders are scheduled locally on your device; the app does not use push
  servers. Optional.

Denying any permission simply disables that one feature.

## Where your data lives

Your data is stored in our backend provider, **Supabase**, which hosts the
database and file storage. Access is protected by row-level security so that
each account can reach only its own data. Data is transmitted over encrypted
connections (HTTPS/TLS).

## Sharing

We do not sell your data and we do not share it with advertisers. Data is
processed only by the infrastructure providers needed to run the app (Supabase
for storage; the app store and, if enabled, Sentry for crash diagnostics).
Crash reports contain technical error information, not your notes, contacts, or
photos.

## Deleting your data

You can delete your account at any time from **Settings → Delete account**. This
permanently removes your account and all associated data — people, occasions,
notes, and uploaded photos — from our backend. The action cannot be undone.

## Children

Kindred is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect
data from them.

## Changes
